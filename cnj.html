Description

In Fall 2013, I took a class called Fundamentals of Newsgathering. In addition to writing several articles, I maintained a weekly blog through which I commented on topics relevant to journalism. For my final project, I investigated the effects of the October 2013 government shutdown on America’s national parks. I contacted national parks from across the country and compiled my findings into an article and a multimedia presentation.
